DIPLOMA PROGRAMMES AT OUR UNIVERSITY

School of Business:
This department offers diploma programs in areas such as Business Administration, Accounting, Marketing, Entrepreneurship, and Office Administration. These programs provide students with practical skills for entry-level positions in various business settings.
School of Technology: :
This department focuses on diploma programs in fields such as Information Technology, Computer Science, Software Development, Networking, Electronics, and Telecommunications. These programs prepare students for careers in technology-related industries.
School of Health Sciences:
Diploma programs in healthcare fields such as Nursing, Medical Assisting, Pharmacy Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology, and Radiologic Technology are offered in this department. These programs train students for roles in healthcare settings.
School of Engineering:
Diploma programs in Engineering Technology, Electrical Engineering Technology, Mechanical Engineering Technology, and Civil Engineering Technology may be offered in this department. These programs provide hands-on training for technician-level positions in engineering industries.

School of Design and Creative Arts:
Diploma programs in Graphic Design, Interior Design, Fashion Design, Multimedia Design, and Photography may be offered in this department. These programs focus on developing artistic and design skills for various creative industries.
School of Education and Social Services:
Diploma programs in Early Childhood Education, Social Work, Counseling, and Human Services may be available in this department. These programs prepare students for roles in education and social service organizations.
School of Trades and Vocational Studies:
This department offers diploma programs in trades such as Automotive Technology, Welding, Carpentry, Plumbing, and HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning). These programs provide hands-on training for skilled trades professions.
School of Culinary Arts:
This department offers diploma programs in Culinary Arts, Baking and Pastry Arts, Hospitality Management, and Food Service Management. These programs train students for careers in the food service and hospitality industries.
