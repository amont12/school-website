ADMISSION


Application requirements


To apply for admission at Mega International University, prospective students must meet the following general requirements:

1. Possess a relevant Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes.


2. Hold a Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 1 principal pass and 2 subsidiary passes obtained at the same sitting.


3. For specific programs, additional requirements may apply, such as specific subject combinations or grades. Applicants are required to obtain an application form from the university or apply online through the university’s admissions portal. The application forms should be filled out accurately and submitted along with the necessary supporting documents, including academic transcripts, certificates, and any other requested materials.


Scholarship


Mega International University offers various scholarship opportunities to both local and international students. These scholarships are usually merit-based or need-based. Some of the common scholarships available include government scholarships, donor-funded scholarships, and institutional scholarships. Students interested in applying for scholarships should check the university’s official website or contact the scholarship office for detailed information on eligibility criteria, application procedures, and deadlines.
