BACHELOR PROGRAMMES AT OUR UNIVERSITY

Faculty of Arts and Humanities:
This faculty encompasses a wide range of disciplines related to human culture, creativity, and expression. It typically includes departments such as English, History, Philosophy, Linguistics, Literature, Fine Arts, Music, Theater, and Cultural Studies.
Faculty of Social Sciences:
This faculty focuses on the study of human society and social relationships. It includes departments such as Sociology, Psychology, Anthropology, Political Science, Economics, Geography, and Communication Studies
Faculty of Science:
This faculty encompasses disciplines related to the natural world and scientific inquiry. It typically includes departments such as Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Environmental Science, and Geology.
Faculty of Engineering:
This faculty focuses on the design, creation, and application of technology and engineering principles. It includes departments such as Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Aerospace Engineering
Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences:
This faculty is dedicated to the study and practice of medicine, healthcare, and related fields. It includes departments such as Medicine, Nursing, Pharmacy, Public Health, Dentistry, Physiotherapy, and Nutrition.
Faculty of Business and Management:
This faculty focuses on the study of business administration, management principles, and organizational dynamics. It includes departments such as Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Human Resource Management, and Entrepreneurship.
Faculty of Law:
This faculty is dedicated to the study of legal principles, jurisprudence, and the application of law in society. It includes departments such as Law, Legal Studies, Criminal Justice, and International Law.
Faculty of Education:
This faculty focuses on the theory and practice of education, teacher training, curriculum development, and educational psychology. It includes departments such as Education, Teaching, Educational Leadership, and Counseling.
Faculty of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences:
This faculty is dedicated to the study of agriculture, sustainable farming practices, environmental conservation, and related fields. It includes departments such as Agriculture, Environmental Science, Forestry, and Wildlife Conservation.
